Why we exist

Why?

Many software systems, especially older systems, have major flaws that can severely limit a company's growth. We help remove those limitations.

Poor Feature Creation

Like a house with a weak foundation, hastily constructed software will stiffle further advances, limiting features and reliability.

Performance and Scale

Poor deployment setups and weak system tuning can prevent software from becoming as reliable and performant as the business requires. Additionally, hidden traps in the code may dramatically clog performance.

Security

Security flaws can have heavy consequences if not dealt with. Older systems are especially susceptible to major exploits, potentially causing severe damages by hackers.

Process

How do we work?

  • Initial Consultation

    We discuss your current software and any problems you're experiencing with it, as well as your goals and needs for where you want the technology to go.

  • Project Outline

    We'll present an outline that will summarize our analysis of the current software, its limitations, and the steps we'll need to take to reach your goals.

  • Work Starts

    Transition to Full Service

    Once project goals are set, we'll start working on the target goals. Progress can be tracked in real time via a code repository, and consultants will be available to answer any questions.

  • Periodic Updates

    Depending on the size of the project and need, there will be periodic updates happening at least weekly, with smaller, quicker daily updates via email.

  • Delivery and Review

    We discuss the end result and make sure the project is completed to specification and your satisfaction
